Choose the Best Loan for You

  • Secured loans

    A secured loan or homeowner loan requires you to put up an asset as collateral, usually a property that you own or pay the mortgage on. If you fail to make your payments, the lender has the right to seize the asset, which means you could lose your home.

  • Unsecured loans

    An unsecured loan, also known as a personal loan, does not require the use of collateral. Lenders look at your financial history to determine whether you qualify and how much you can borrow; it helps if you have a good credit score and have previously kept up with debt repayments.
